What We Do

Espresso House is the largest coffeehouse chaingin the Nordic countries. At Espresso House we are passionate about a wonderful coffee experience. brewed coffee and espresso served in our coffee shops is a so-called special coffee. Roasting, beans, quality and aromas are important factors for us to be able to proudly serve our guests a really good cup of coffee.

All this, however, ends up in the shadows if we can not proudly say that the coffee is grown under healthy conditions. Crucial for the coffee to come to its right taste is also the freshness. It is precisely freshly roasted coffee that has the most delicious flavors and aromas. Membership card

If you have a Coffee Card that you have filled up with a sufficient amount, you can use the card when you pay at the checkout in our coffee shops. When you use your Coffee Card, you get a 10% discount on selected food and beverages, and when you pay, the amount is deducted from your Coffee Card.

You can visit all of our coffee shops and request a Coffee Card. The card is not personal and is completely free.
