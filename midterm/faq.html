Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know what size to order?

We know it can be hard to determine your size when online shopping. The best way to get an accurate fit is to go by measurements, not just age or random size numbers that vary from brand to brand. Click here for a complete guide on our unique sizing. If your child is in between sizes, we usually recommend buying a size larger; the extra room won’t be there for long!

Are larger sizes available?

At the moment, we are catering to all-sized feet.

My child has chubby feet. Will Shoey shoes shoes fit him?

Yes! Our chubby-footed beta testers can attest to this! Certain “open” styles will fit better than others (we’ve found the booties can be challenging with the most extreme cases). In addition, you can remove the insoles which will increase the volume of the shoe and accommodate your child’s fuller foot better.

Is there variation in fit between the different styles?

All Fields Footwear shoes are made on the same last, or mold upon which the shoe is constructed. With the exception of extremely chubby feet, if your child fits any one Fields Footwear style, they should fit them all just as well!
