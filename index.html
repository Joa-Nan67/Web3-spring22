Whiz Kid

Whiz Kid center for intelligence is a science summer camp. Children of ages 10 and above can come and
spend the summer learning about robots, space and,
other science topics. This is a 8 week program Starting july 1st every year and applications are open from January 1st to May 25th

Our values and
intentions

Inspiring young minds

The science field scary for alot of people to tap in, that is why for us it is so important that we show kids how fun, easy and Rewarding the science world can be.

Educating

Since technology and science is the future, we focus on educating young minds about different aspects of science to stimulate there minds and give them an understanding about simple physics and math.

Play

We understand that our program is during the summer, a time for play dates and ice cream. We would think of deprving our kids of Fun thats why the Children’s schedule has hours dedicated to playing and having fun amongst other things.

Friendships

Summer camp is all about fun, trying new things, and making friendships that will last a lifetime. There’s something undeniably special about summer camp friendships since they last long after camp has ended.
